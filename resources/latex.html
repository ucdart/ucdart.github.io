Refer to the Wikipedia entry for basic information on LaTeX. A great introductory tutorials on LaTeX is The Not So Short Introduction to LaTeX2e by Tobi Oetiker. The LaTeX Wikibook is also a great start point. Intermediate level users may find "Guide to LaTeX: Tools and Techniques for Computer Typesetting" by Helmut Kopka and Patrick W. Daly useful.
